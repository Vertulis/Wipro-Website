About us

Company History

Founded in 1945 by Mohamed Premji as Western India Vegetable Products Limited, Wipro began as a manufacturer of cooking oils. In the 1980s, under the leadership of Azim Premji, the company ventured into the IT industry, establishing itself as a global leader in technology services and consulting.
