Industries

Wipro serves a diverse range of industries, offering tailored solutions to address specific challenges within each sector. Below is an overview of major industries Wipro serves, the common challenges they face, Wipro's solutions to these challenges, and example case studies illustrating the impact of these solutions.

1. Utilities

Challenges:

  • Complex integration scenarios due to business restructuring.
  • Need for seamless and secure data exchange over the internet.
  • Disparate systems leading to higher operational costs and reduced performance.

Wipro's Solutions:

  • Implementation of customized utilities solutions.
  • Leveraging Managed File Transfer as-a-Service (MFTaaS) for secure data exchange.
  • Consolidation of disparate business systems into unified workflows.

Case Study:

Wipro assisted a client in the utilities sector by implementing its MFTaaS solution, enabling guaranteed and secure data exchange between market operators and the client's wholesale and retail businesses. This integration led to accelerated business processes and compliance with new regulatory changes in England's retail water market.
