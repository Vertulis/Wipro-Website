Our Tech Highlights

AI-Powered Data Management for Telecom

In collaboration with BT Group, Wipro utilized artificial intelligence to create a mobile tower infrastructure database. This solution automated the extraction and migration of essential data—such as lease agreements, site surveys, and antenna specifications—into a centralized platform. The implementation enhanced data management efficiency and led to significant cost savings.

Industry 4.0 Solutions

Wipro's Industry 4.0 offerings enable enterprises to accelerate time-to-market, improve profitability, reduce carbon footprints, and adapt to changing market demands. For instance, their on-demand product engineering solution, Product POD, addresses the challenge of balancing innovation with current market needs, thereby enhancing customer satisfaction and market share.

Cloud Transformation and Cybersecurity

Wipro has showcased solutions in cloud transformation, modernization, cybersecurity, data analytics, and artificial intelligence. Their investments in Wipro FullStride Cloud Services have become essential for businesses, emphasizing the importance of cloud services in modern enterprises.

Spatial Computing and Extended Reality (XR) Solutions

Wipro is exploring spatial computing to enhance customer service, shopping experiences, travel, healthcare interactions, and remote work. Additionally, they are developing XR and AI-powered solutions aimed at improving safety, environmental protection, and operational efficiency.
